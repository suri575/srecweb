ECE Department Labs

As we believe that laboratory mirrors the knowledge and its experience will be a valuable part of engineering education, ECE Department has been designed to provide the students with the basic laboratory skills needed in engineering profession. The ability to build and evaluate the performance of electronics and communications equipment is essential for acquiring the knowledge in Electronics and Communications Engineering. Laboratory assignments are designed to emphasize the comprehensive skills on key concepts, rigorous analysis of electronic circuits and the presentation of results. Department Laboratorieswith focused research groups, are working in areas of communication and Electronics. The Department laboratory equipped with all kind of software’s and electronic systems to carry research relevant to the current technologies and needs of industry. To gain the experience from laboratory one must study each experiment and answer the pre-lab questions in advance of the laboratory meeting. A diligent effort to synchronize experiments with lecture topics has been made. students may be required at times to perform experiments on topics that have been fully covered in class. It is therefore important to be well prepared for each lab session by starting the pre-lab exercise early and asking questions as necessary. ​

APPLIED PHYSICS LAB (Common to I B.Tech I/II Seme(EEE, ECE, CSE & CSE-DS )
OBJECTIVES:
  • Understands the concepts of interference, diffraction and their applications.
  • Understand the role of optical fiber parameters in communication.
  • Recognize the importance of energy gap in the study of conductivity and Hall Effect in a semiconductor.
  • To provide practical understand and extend the knowledge on basic concepts and applications of semiconductor based electronic devices, and magnetic materials which find potential in the emerging micro device applications.
  • Will understand the applications of B-H curve.
  • Understand intensity of the magnetic field of circular coil carrying current with distance.
  • Will understand dielectric constant by charging and discharging method.
List of Experiments:
  1. Understands the concepts of interference, diffraction and their applications.
  2. Determine the thickness of the wire using wedge shape method
  3. Determination of the radius of curvature of the lens by Newton’s ring method
  4. Determination of wavelength by plane diffraction grating method
  5. Determination of dispersive power of prism.
  6. Determination of wavelength of LASER light using diffraction grating.
  7. Determination of particle size using LASER.
  8. To determine the numerical aperture of a given optical fiber and hence to find its acceptance angle
  9. Determination of dielectric constant by charging and discharging method.
  10. Magnetic field along the axis of a circular coil carrying current –Stewart Gee’s method.
  11. Measurement of magnetic susceptibility by Gouy’s method
  12. Study the variation of B versus H by magnetizing the magnetic material (B-H curve)
  13. To determine the resistivity of semiconductor by Four probe method
  14. To determine the energy gap of a semiconductor
  15. Determination of Hall voltage and Hall coefficient of a given semiconductor using Hall Effect.
  16. Measurement of resistance with varying temperature.

OBJECTIVES:
  • Operate optical instruments like microscope and spectrometer
  • Determine thickness of a hair/paper with the concept of interference Estimate the wavelength of different colors using diffraction grating and resolving power Plot the intensity of the magnetic field of circular coil carrying current with distance
  • Evaluate the acceptance angle of an optical fiber and numerical aperture Determine the resistivity of the given semiconductor using four probe methods.
  • Identify the type of semiconductor i.e., n-type or p-type using hall effect
  • Calculate the band gap of a given semiconductor

6. Master of Business Administration

MBA Department
Department Profile

The growth of industrialization demands the expert managers. This was that boosted SREC to introduce management course. The department of MBA was switched on in the year 2009 with an intake of 60 that was increased to 120 from the year 2014. The department offers post graduation along with the specializations such as Human Resources, Marketing and Finance. The department has experienced and dedicated faculty as one of its major assets.
The department works towards providing career based education in the field of management and encourages the students to become entrepreneurs. In this connection the department provides students interactions with industrialist and visits to the industries. To enhance management skills of the students the department also conducts various programmes like management meets, seminars, workshops, guest lecturers etc under students’ club activities naming it “SYNERGY”.

Vision and Mission
Department Vision
  • To contribute societal sustainable development through providing professional, entrepreneurial and socially responsible global managers.
Department Mission
  • To provide career-oriented education in the field of management T

POs and COs

  3. Ability to develop value based leadership ability.

  5. Ability to lead themselves and others in the achievement of organisational contributing effectively to a team environment.

  • To increase the competitive nature among student groups.
  • To develop the team spirit.

Computer Center

The central computer lab in SREC is to cater the research and learning needs of the students and staff. Whoever associated to SREC can be given permission to access the systems at this lab. The lab is installed with 60 systems of high configuration; all our students and scholars make use of this facility for preparing their research works.

Laboratories

All the computer labs in SREC are connected with a LAN of high speed internet facility besides updated and licensed software installations. The other labs are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment. A technician was entrusted in each lab to monitor the equipments regularly.

Seminar Halls

A centralized air conditioned and well-furnished conference hall with all required audio-visual facilities is another countable asset to SREC. The conference hall has the seating capacity of holding 500+ audiences with a clear view of the stage area. Clear and high-definition audio visual aids are arranged for achieving digital clarity in voice and display. Guest lectures, seminars, Dept meets, other programs like workshops etc are held very comfortably in the conference hall.
