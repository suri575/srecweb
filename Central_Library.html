Central Library

Information is an essential input to national development. The modern library has great responsibility in harnessing and utilization of information. The library and information centers strive for high quality of library services which emphasis the actual delivery of information rather than actual delivery of documents for meeting their generic as well as specific needs of the users. “Knowledge is everything, but everything can’t provide knowledge like a Book”. The Central Library in SREC plays an important role in the life and career of students. It is computerized, well spaced and provided with all the facilities reaching the students’ needs. The Digital library facilitates the Students and Faculty to access a number of Journals, Books and Magazines online. The college library housed in an area of about 620 sq.m., is a nerve center of activity. It has a collection of 4242 book titles & 26440 book volumes, 58 E-Books & it subscribes to 223 journals (national, International & E Journals) besides a number of dailies & periodicals. Library has DELNET (Inter Library Loan) for resource sharing and it is fully computerized with bar-coding system.

Rules & regulations

  • The following instructions are to be followed strictly by the students who use the library facilities.
  • Every student has to sign in to the daily gate register before entry.
  • Identification card has to be shown at the entrance gate whenever necessary.
  • Strict silence should be maintained in the library and in the reading hall.
  • Tracing and disfiguring books are strictly forbidden.
  • Books shall be issued only on the production of the Identity Cards.
  • Books borrowed shall be returned in good condition before specified dates (14 Days) failing which a fine shall be levied at the rates given below :
    • First week after due date Second Week
    Rs. 1/- per day including holidays Rs. 2/- per day including holidays
  • Borrower’s are responsible for every book taken by them for books damaged, torn of pages, defaced or lost.
  • If anybody is found taking away the books from the library before they are issued by the library staff, severe disciplinary action will be initiated against such a person.
  • Personal belongings such as hand-bags, printed books shall not be allowed inside the library, but allowed to be places at the property counter.
  • Reference books are not permitted for leading for use outside the library.
  • Library CDs will not be issued for outside use.
