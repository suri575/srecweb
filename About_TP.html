Training & Placement Cell of SREC provides a window of opportunities to the Students over a wide variety of careers in a broad spectrum of Industries/Organizations in India and Abroad. It serves as a single point of contact for the Organizations on various Industry-Institute Interactions.

The main objective of the cell is to mould the students to meet the corporate expectation and place them in reputed companies based on the expected Job profiles of each student. Special training is given to every student from 1st year to 4th year which covers, communication skills, logical analysis, analytical analysis, HR, Latest Technologies, and GD’s. This helps the student to improve the opportunities of placements.

Placement Cell Activities

We have a full-fledged Placement cell, which monitors the employment opportunities and arrange campus interviews for the final year students.

  • Our Campus recruitment program starts at the beginning of the 3rd Year..
  • The On-Campus Recruitment program keeps continuing till the end of their final semester.
  • And after that, we will be helping our students by arranging Off-Campus Interviews also.
  • Students are not encouraged to participate in the further campus drives once they are placed through campus selection conducted by college, FOR THE LOWER PACKAGES.
  • Our management is having a strong policy not to encourage the companies to do Education & Training activities to attract the students in the name of placement orders.
