Our Deans & Directors

Deans generally have broader responsibilities concerning academic governance and the overall mission of their faculties or schools, while Directors tend to focus on managing specific units or initiatives within the institution.

Dr. B. Haritha - Dean of AAP

She completed UG (B.Sc-Computers) in the year 2005, PG (M.Sc-Mathematics) in the year 2007 from SK Univrsity, Ananthappur, and PhD in the year 2018 from JNTUA, Ananthapur. She has 16+ years of experience in teaching and research. She published 1 WOS, 2 SCOPUS, 13 other research papers in various national and international journals and conferences. She also has ONE design patent grant and TWO published patents in her credit. She also published a book entitled “Numerical Methods”. Guiding one PhD scholars in JNTUA.

Dean – Research & Development
Dr. G. Sowmya - Dean of R&D

She completed UG (B.Tech-EIE) and PG (M.Tech-ECE) from JNTUA, Ananthapuramu, and Ph.D in the year 2022 from KL University, Guntur. She has 15+ years of experience in teaching and research. She published 6 WOS, 12 SCOPUS and 15 other research papers in various national and international journals and conferences. She has published 12 patents and also published a book entitled “Information Technology for Cyber security”. She received ONE Minor Project from IE(I), DST-NIMAT, seed money from IFERP, FDP from JNTUA. She also received “Best Teacher Award” from KXC, “Student Mentorship & Development Award” from Bharat Education Excellence Awards. She acted as the Coordinator-Skill course from PMKVY, NSDC. In addition to the role of Dean-R&D, she is also acting as the Coordinator-IIC, which received 4* rating from the Ministry of Education.
